Estancia Cafetera

A volcanic hospitality system

Coffee is not the product. Presence is the product. A 14-day case study in designing hospitality as a system — from atmosphere to interface — using AI as infrastructure.

By Julissa Roa Hospitality Brand & Experience Designer June 2026

Plan Surfaces & Slot Inventory

Deciding where the work lives before deciding what fills it.

  1. 05

    Deliverable Surfaces

    Four surfaces locked: Figma Make marketing site · Instagram launch grid with motion reels · Guest Portal welcome screen · Figma Weave motion hero. Each chosen because it serves a different proof of the positioning.

    Done
  2. 06

    Wireframes

    Lo-fi sketches for the five Figma Make frames: Hero · Philosophy · The Place · Ritual · Stay. Inverting the hospitality category convention by putting philosophy second and booking last.

    Active
  3. 07

    Asset Map

    Every image and motion slot mapped to its surface. Reuse counted. Final count: ten to twelve unique stills plus one motion loop — eighty-five percent less generation than the original plan called for.
